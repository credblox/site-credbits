Build your first blockchain app in 5 mins!

App-focused blockchain framework

Building a blockchain app usually involves a steep learning curve, taxing infrastructure requirements and gets highly complex.

Credbits is a fast growing framework for blockchain development with a focus of ease of development and one-click deployment of blockchain artifacts.

How it works

Once you clone the credbits repo, you can...

1Define your digital asset
At the heart of every credbits-based application is an asset that needs to be traced and viewed transparently.

For instance, one that describes an organic product in an organic supply chain tracing app, a news article in a fake news detection app and so on.

Credbits developers get a jumpstart as they focus on defining their core asset that drives business value rather than working on bare metal infra setup.
2Build blockchain network
Right from defining channels for secure communication between involved participants to deploying smart contracts, all low-level blockchain operations are handled by the framework so you're up and running in just a few clicks.

Once you have the network up and running, you can start deploying and running your custom smart contract to enable transactions on your asset.

3Transact with transparency
By allowing verifiable open source code at each level of access,

transactions involving your blockchain assets will not only be tamper proof, but also highly transparent, taking consumer and multi-party trust to unprecedented levels.
